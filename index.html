Brief CV

Currently I am an associate professor of Department of Cognitive Science and Technology in  Xiamen University. I received my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in  Computer Science  from Wuhan University in June 2001 and June 2007, respectively. From 2005 to 2007, I was a visiting student at Department of Computer Science of City university of Hong Kong.  I am an IEEE Senior Member (MIEEE'201l, SMIEEE'2012). Here is a full version of my CV.

Research Interests

My current research interests mainly include Machine Learning and Computational Intelligence.  More specifically, I am interested in: Transfer Learning, Deep Learning, (Dynamic) Multi/Many-objective Optimization, Estimation of Distribution Algorithms and their applications on Intelligent Robotics.
